About

Mayfair Design Studio is a young and dynamic full-service architecture and hospitality interior design firm, specializing in homes, developments and hospitality venues including clubs, hotels and restaurant interior design.

Whether handling private or commercial interiors, our hospitality interior design services are comprehensive, encompassing concept design, construction documentation, tender and construction-stage services. Because building projects require a range of specialist hospitality interior design consultants, we maintain a number of industry contacts that can provide expert advice. We take pride in working with our clients throughout the entire process to ensure satisfaction.

APPROACH

At Mayfair Design Studio we are committed to delivering consistent quality of service and excellence in design.

Regardless of budget, our first step is to build an understanding with the client and interpret their vision in order to make every scheme unique and distinctive.

A design team working to a detailed specification system, which documents every aspect of the design process, carefully manages each project. It is this meticulous attention to detail, applied to all projects, that is the Mayfair Design trademark.

Our project resume includes hotels, resorts, and private residences, show flats, independent restaurants, clubs and corporate offices. Recognizing the paramount constraints of schedule and budget, Studio delivers design solutions that are memorable yet responsive to real world project constraints.
